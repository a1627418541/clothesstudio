# Style Studio Logo Design

## Objective

Create a distinctive wordmark for Style Studio that matches the product's high-fashion editorial interface and works as the primary desktop web header identity.

## Approved Direction

Use an editorial contrast wordmark with the exact brand text `STYLE STUDIO`.

- `STYLE` is the visual lead: uppercase, high-contrast editorial serif, tall and refined.
- `STUDIO` is secondary: smaller uppercase sans serif with generous tracking.
- A fine oxblood divider separates the two words.
- The composition is horizontal and optimized for the desktop website header.
- The identity is typographic only. It has no standalone symbol or pictogram.

## Brand Palette

- Ink: `#151311`
- Oxblood accent: `#7B2636`
- Paper background: `#F4EFE7`
- Reversed mark: paper or white lettering on ink

## Visual Character

The mark should feel like a contemporary fashion magazine masthead: restrained, cultured, precise, premium, and editorial. It should complement the existing Cormorant Garamond and Geist typography without appearing like unmodified typeset text.

## Composition Rules

- Preserve the exact spelling `STYLE STUDIO`.
- Keep `STYLE` larger and more prominent than `STUDIO`.
- Use a narrow vertical or subtly angled divider in oxblood.
- Keep generous breathing room and optical alignment between both word groups.
- Avoid excessive flourishes so the mark remains legible at navigation-header size.

## Prohibited Elements

- No hanger, garment, mannequin, face, body silhouette, sparkle, AI star, magic wand, monogram, or enclosing badge.
- No gradients, shadows, three-dimensional effects, photographic texture, or decorative background scene.
- No tagline, watermark, mock company name, or additional lettering.

## Preview Deliverable

Produce one clean brand presentation image showing:

1. Primary ink-and-oxblood wordmark on the paper background.
2. Monochrome black version.
3. Reversed light-on-ink version.

The presentation is for visual approval. Final production assets should later be recreated as deterministic vector/SVG artwork so typography and alignment remain exact.

## Acceptance Criteria

- The wordmark reads exactly `STYLE STUDIO`.
- It visually belongs to the current high-fashion editorial web UI.
- It remains recognizable and legible in a desktop navigation header.
- The three color variants share identical proportions and typography.
- No prohibited symbol or decorative effect appears.
